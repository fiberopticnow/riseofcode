Rise of //Code
No other skills will be as valuable to 21st century students than tech skills, and programming.
Rise of //Code aims to provide Dutch students with the opportunity to get hands-on experience
with technology like programming, hardware, 3D printing, and more, as well as get mentorship
from young tech-stars from university, and the Netherland's booming tech startup scene.
