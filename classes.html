RISE OF //CODE

No other skills will be as valuable to 21st century students than tech skills, and programming.
RISE OF //CODE is a new initative that offers Dutch students the opportunity to get hands-on experience
with technology like programming, hardware, 3D printing, and more!
Even better, RISE OF //CODE teachers are real tech professionals from Amsterdam's startup scene.
We believe that students should always learn from the best!
